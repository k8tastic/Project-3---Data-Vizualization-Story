Key considerations for buying a foreclosure: It'll blow your mind.

1. Make sure you can get the cash. Successful bidders will have to pay the full amount within a few days. 

2. Don't bank on one property. Oftentimes you will get out bid or the foreclosure will be cancelled. Plan to be flexible! 

3. Understand the risks. You can't see the inside, or predict when the previous owners will leave, so there are many unknowns. Given that the worst case scenario can happen, you must be prepared to lose money.  

4. Use good data. DMV Foreclosure Notices features real time foreclosure data that will save you hours of combing the classifieds yourself.

  A.  Only buy properties that have a good amount of equity. 

  • Properties before 2000 and during the crisis (2007 to 2009) probably have good equity. 

  B.  Buy in areas that you know. 

  C.  Get to the auction on time.
